# Local Fresh Eyes

Load and follow the `fresh-eyes-local` skill.

Pass `$ARGUMENTS` through as local Fresh Eyes options when they match the skill's
documented script flags, especially `--repo-path <path>` and `--base <ref>`.

Do not push from this command.
